# CiteDom
**Help brands get cited by AI—not just ranked in traditional search.**
[CiteDom](https://citedom.com) builds tools for **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**—also called AEO or conversational SEO—so teams can understand and improve how ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and similar systems discover and cite their content.
---
### What we focus on
- **Citation-oriented quality** — We evaluate sites against the **GEO-16** framework: sixteen on-page signals that empirical work links to being cited by AI answer engines.
- **Research-backed, not guesswork** — Our methodology draws on peer-reviewed work in GEO, citation behavior, and competitive dynamics in AI-mediated search.
- **Actionable output** — Analysis turns into prioritized recommendations you can ship, not vague “write better content” advice.
